 1. Copy the contents of GameData to your JKA GameData directory.
 2. Create a shortcut on your desktop to Warzone.x86.exe.
 3. Right click the new desktop icon and go to properties.
 4. Change the "Target" field to "<INSERT_YOUR_JKA_EXE_DIR_HERE>\Warzone.x86.exe" +set cl_renderer rd-warzone +set fs_game warzone
 5. Double click the icon. Play the game!
